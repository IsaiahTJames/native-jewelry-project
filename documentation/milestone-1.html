Milestone 1 Documentation

Purpose

The purpose of this personal project is to create a professional website for my aunt's Native American jewelry and apparel business. This site will serve as a platform to showcase her handmade products, and creativity to online consumers.

Audience

This website is to target at individuals who are interested in jewelry and Native American culture, particularly those who are located in the Southwest, such as New Mexico. The audience also includes consumers who value authentic, handcrafted products and those who seek for culturally significant items that represent Native American heritage.

Goal

The primary goal of this site to showcase the online visibility for my aunt's business, boost product sales, and establish a professional online presence. Secondary goals is to provide an elegant and visually appealing side of Navajo culture and jewelry, offering a glimpse into its craftsmanship and artistry.

Persona

Name: Jessica R. Martinez

Age: 36

Profession:

Jessica is an experienced accountant specializing in financial management for small businesses. On the side, she has a passion for photography, fashion, and aesthetics, often spending weekends at exhibits or doing her own freelance photography projects. Her creative hobbies help her balance her professional life with a sense of artistic fulfillment.

Technology:

  • Work: Windows-based workstation with dual monitors from her accountant career.
  • Personal: MacBook Air and an iPhone 14 Pro used for creative projects, including photo editing software like Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

  • Organized and detail-oriented, balancing her analytical job with her artisan passions.
  • Curious and open-minded, always seeking opportunities to learn and grow both professionally and personally.
  • Values meaningful connections and interactions, whether through her career or personal hobbies.
  • Enjoys the outdoors and keeping a healthy lifestyle since she spends many hours behind a screen and indoors when she is working.
  • Goal-driven, with a strong sense of staying on task when pursuing her won personal and professional ambitions.
  • Time-conscious, she knows what she likes and wants to be straight to the point because she structures out her day.

Frustrations and Needs:

  • Frustrated with excessive screen time and its impact on her physical and mental well-being.
  • Frustrated by websites that are difficult to navigate, slow, or cluttered with unnecessary content.
  • Dislikes generic shopping options where she knows what she wants but its sometimes difficult to find.
  • Feels disconnected from traditional creativity since she is surrounded by technology from a day-to-day basis.
  • Overwhelmed by too many online options when searching for high-quality, authentic, and meaningful products.
  • Seeks an intuitive, smooth online shopping experience where she can easily browse and discover what she likes.
  • Seeks an eye-catching and visually appealing website that highlights products and is straightforward.
  • Needs a fast-loading website to avoid frustration and ensure a pleasant online shopping experience.
  • Requires a mobile-friendly design, so she is able to explore seamlessly on other devices when she is not at a desktop or her macbook.
  • Wants personalized recommendation based on her history and preferences to simplify her own decision-making.

Goals:

Jessica's primary goal is to have a seamless product discovery experience, allowing her to find what she needs quickly without navigating through unnecessary options. She values a visually appealing and engaging interface that captures her artistic interest. Convenience is key, so Jessica prefers quick access to information, clear product description and a painless checkout process. While time management is important, she appreciates the opportunity to explore interests further when inspired. Personalized recommendations based on her preferences would help her make faster, more satisfying decisions. Lastly, with Jessica's active lifestyle and use of multiple devices, she requires a mobile-friendly design that allows her to browse website easily from her iPhone or MacBook, even when she's on the go.

User-Story:

As a user, I need an intuitive and visually appealing online store that allows me to discover authentic, handcrafted Native American jewelry and apparel effortlessly. I want to be able to browse products efficiently, with a clear display and images that showcase the products on the website. Additionally, I need the site to be fast, mobile-friendly, and easy to navigate, so I can shop conveniently on my iPhone or MacBook whenever I have time to myself.
