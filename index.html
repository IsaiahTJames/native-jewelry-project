Love Adorned

Love Adorned by Lori Tazbah (Diné/Acoma) offers handmade, contemporary Indigenized jewelry crafted from antiques, recycled materials, and natural stones. Lori blends Native culture with modern fashion, creating unique, one-of-a-kind pieces of art. Love Adorned also features custom decals and prints by Native pop artist Steven Paul Judd (Choctaw/Kiowa) celebrating Indigenous artistry and creativity.

Love Adorned by Lori Tazbah (Diné/Acoma) specializes in handmade contemporary Indigenized antiques. Her modern take on Native jewelry uses antiques, recycled, and repurposed materials paired with natural stones and shells. Each piece is a fusion between Lori's Native culture and mainstream fashion, resulting in unique, one-of-a-kind, Indigenized pieces of art. Love Adorned also exclusively carries custom decals and prints by Native pop artist, Steven Paul Judd (Choctaw/Kiowa).
